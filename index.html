Health & Veterinary Care

Chulites™

A modular device that dogs chew, emitting wavelength-specific light that eliminates pathogens causing canine periodontitis, dog owners' largest medical expense, affecting the majority of dogs.

Patented #11,122,775
Chulites robust core contains safe NiMH batteries and LEDs that emit 405 nm light, an FDA authorized visible violet wavelength that eliminates gum disease bacteria like Porphyromonas and Tannerella. A replaceable chew-toy housing surrounds the core. Designed as a home-use veterinary medical device, not a toy, it has a clinical validation path.

Athletic Safety & Biomechanics

Biomechanically Realistic Brain Model™

A layered physical brain model designed to study how rotational and impact loading affect gray and white matter under realistic test conditions.

Patent Pending & Published
No standards define brain injury for helmets, since you can't inspect a player's brain after impact. The model brain, placed in a skull form on an impact tester, subjected to controlled collisions, provides the needed data. Thin layer molds, designed from hundreds of brain scan slices, cast 5 distinct materials, each mimicking a brain tissue, are interleaved in realistic brain architecture, instrumented for analysis.
Public & Environmental Safety

Siren Guide™

A metamaterial wavefront-shaping adapter that directs emergency vehicle siren sound at street level while reducing noise in multistory offices and apartments.

Patented #11,202,144
Siren noise is the number-one public complaint in dense cities like New York, causes hypertension, and decreases property values. Siren Guide attaches directly to an existing siren horn and targets sound energy where it is needed.
Athletic Safety & Biomechanics

Steadihelm™

A Halbach-array magnetic damping system that actively counters rotational forces transmitted to the head during impact — addressing the root cause of concussion.

Patented #11,464,270
Steadihelm arrays magnetic dipoles between a helmet shell and an inner head cover. When a collision drives the helmet one way, negative torque drives the head cover the opposite direction.

Agricultural Biotechnology

ABC Capsule™ & Triboelectric Energizer

A self-powered cow capsule that harvests rumen motion to generate electricity for limited bacteria reduction that mimics prophylactic antibiotic productivity boost without triggering antibiotic resistance.

ABC Capsule Patented #11,800,851 Triboelectric Energizer Patent Pending
The use of prophylactic antibiotics in cows reduces bacteria populations by a few percent; Soret band light can do this without promoting resistance. The triboelectric device, cow-magnet sized, harvests powerful rumen movement to power LEDs or any in-vivo sensors for the life of the animal.
Health & Veterinary Care

Motion Control Capsule (MCap™)

A controllable capsule concept for the gastrointestinal tract that uses light-responsive material behavior to slow, stop, or manage movement.

Patented #11,883,007 &
A steerable colonoscopy capsule that medical professionals can slow or stop with light-activated hydrogel expansion, similar to the way foods that expand can cause constipation. When light deactivates, the hydrogel shrinks and capsule motion continues. MCap is fail safe: if power fails, or the hydrogel detaches, it shrinks and passes. The second patent is a tether system with retrieval capability and real-time positional tracking, enabling the operator to navigate the capsule with physician-level intent and pause at any point of interest for detailed imaging.

Public & Environmental Safety

Level 3 Fire Detector (L3FD™)

An electric tower thermopile fire-detection system that detects wildfire ignition by infrared wavelength analysis, catching fires early enough to prevent spread, at low infrastructure cost.

Patented #12,424,068
Current fire detection are reported when they become visible to people or aircraft, or from expensive, theft-prone infrared cameras. L3FD is low cost, easily deployed, and has no alternative resale value. Mounted on existing communication or utility towers, each L3FD unit uses an array of thermopile sensors to passively detect the specific infrared wavelength signatures of active flame, and not false positives.
Public & Environmental Safety

Butterfly Covers™

A disposable N95 cover designed to reduce contamination risk during removal by enabling a controlled fold-away motion, preventing viral shedding.

Patent Pending
Butterfly Covers are designed to fold off the face rather than snap or peel, which generates mechanical agitation that disperses virions. Folding keeps the contaminated surface facing inward, trapping particles as the cover is removed. During pandemics, crisis conditions prevent adoption of new innovations. Butterfly Covers are best introduced and stockpiled during ordinary times.
